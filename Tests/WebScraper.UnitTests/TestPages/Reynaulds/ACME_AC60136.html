    Product Features and Details
       

    Model: The Acme 60136 electric locomotive class E646 is a very impressive model with exceptional running performance and extensive detail. The model is equipped with a powerful 5 pole motor with flywheel, full cab interiors, sprung loaded pantographs, intricate roof details and much more. Model also has LED headlights that coordinate with direction of travel and all the modern technology available on the market today. Add an Acme model to your collection today.

    Model Details:

    • 5 Pole Motor with Flywheel

    • Directional Lighting

    • LED Lighting

    • NEM Coupler Pockets

    • NEM 652 Digital Decoder Receptacle
